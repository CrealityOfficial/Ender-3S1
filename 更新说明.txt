
屏幕更新说明：
    1. 将文件夹“DisplayScreenFirmware”下的文件和文件夹放入TF卡根目录
    2. 将tf卡插入屏幕卡槽。
    3. 重启机器等待完成。

主板更新：
   1. 将主板固件的STM32F4_UPDATE文件夹放在SD卡根目录
   2. 将SD卡插入机器卡槽
   3. 重启等待更新完成。


Screen update instructions:
     1. Put the files and folders under the folder "DisplayScreenFirmware" into the root directory of the TF card
     2. Insert the TF card into the screen card slot.
     3. Restart the machine and wait for completion.

Board update:
    1. Put the STM32F4_UPDATE folder of the motherboard firmware in the root directory of the SD card
    2. Insert the SD card into the card slot of the machine
    3. Restart and wait for the update to complete.
